TechLearn India was founded in 2020 with a mission to provide affordable technology education to students across India. The company started with just 3 courses and has now grown to offer 20 plus courses in various domains including machine learning, web development, data science and cloud computing.

Our refund policy allows customers to request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After the 30 day period no refunds will be issued under any circumstances. To initiate a refund request customers must email support@techlearn.in with their order ID and reason for refund. Refunds are processed within 7 business days.

The support team at TechLearn India is available Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM IST. We are closed on all public holidays and weekends. For urgent queries customers can email support@techlearn.in or call our helpline at +91-9876543210. We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours.

Enrollment in any course is simple and straightforward. Visit our website at www.techlearn.in select your desired course and complete the payment process. You will receive instant access to the course material after payment confirmation. All courses include lifetime access meaning you can learn at your own pace without any time pressure.
